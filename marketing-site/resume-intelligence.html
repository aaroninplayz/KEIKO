Every Great Interview Starts With Understanding.

Before asking a single question, KEIKO meticulously studies the candidate's resume to build a profound understanding of their experience, skills, education, projects, and achievements.

Context Creates Better Conversations

Interviews fail when they feel like generic interrogations. KEIKO transforms the dynamic by deeply ingesting the applicant's history, allowing for highly relevant, targeted discussions that respect the candidate's actual background.

Designed for Understanding, Not Judgment

KEIKO explicitly does not auto-reject candidates or determine final outcomes autonomously. The Intelligence Profile is designed solely to equip the interviewer (whether human or AI) with the deepest possible context to conduct a fair, rigorous, and relevant conversation.
