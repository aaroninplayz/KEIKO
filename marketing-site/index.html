The Core Boundary

Traditional interviews observe answers.
KEIKO observes understanding.

Standard technical screens evaluate simple memorized facts and syntax. But true engineering competency requires managing concurrent threads of knowledge simultaneously: technical depth, structural communication, composed posture alignment, gaze focus, and vocal speed ratios.

Evaluating all of these channels simultaneously is outside human cognitive bounds. KEIKO combines multiple specialized AI intelligence systems to evaluate every important dimension consistently.

Memory & Context

We evaluate patterns, not moments.

Human interviewers are subject to recency effects, locking onto a single stammer or one highly polished answer. KEIKO monitors the complete timeline of the conversation, analyzing problem-solving patterns that persist across the entire dialogue.

Cognitive Tracking

We observe behavior, not just answers.

A correct answer is only one signal. KEIKO analyzes eye contact stability, head tilt angles, posture, and verbal hesitation rates to verify candidates' ease of expression and focus under technical complexity.

Analytical Explanability

We produce insights, not opinions.

Traditional interview feedbacks end with vague statements like "I liked their vibe." KEIKO outputs clear, explainable insights with direct citations linking back to transcript nodes and telemetry frames.

Confidentiality

Designed for growth, not evaluation.

We believe candidate preparation shouldn't feel like a corporate screen. KEIKO offers offline local model execution (Ollama integration) so you can practice completely privately.
